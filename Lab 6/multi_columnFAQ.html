Innovate Hackathon

FAQ's (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • What is innovate and how does it work?

    Innovate is a 24-hour hackathon with the goal of uniting talented developers and designers under one roof and encouraging healthy competition among them. With the help of a committed and knowledgeable technical staff and a panel of equally stimulating and creative subjects, participants can put their talents to the test and use the results to create projects that address real-world issues
